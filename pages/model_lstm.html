Model Summary

LSTM uses a stacked architecture (64→32 units) with 24-hour sliding sequences to learn non-linear temporal dependencies from historical demand and engineered features. The model achieves excellent performance with MAE: 695.8, RMSE: 903.4, and R²: 0.992, significantly outperforming the SARIMA baseline. It demonstrates 92.00% directional accuracy, indicating strong capability in tracking both demand direction and magnitude.

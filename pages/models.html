Summary

SARIMA is the seasonal baseline and underfits (MAE ~11.5k, R2 ≈ -0.84), while LSTM captures non-linear patterns well (MAE ~765, R2 ≈ 0.99). Hybrid performs worst here (MAE ~16.5k), indicating residual learning did not generalize in this setup. Best fit ensemble is the switch-based model (`final_switch_tuned_q083`) with the lowest MAE (~577) and RMSE (~740) and the highest R2 (~0.994).
